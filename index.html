Science-backed strategies

Ways to ease your mind

Small, gentle actions that genuinely help.

🌿

Ground yourself 5-4-3-2-1

Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Anchors your mind to the present.

💧

Cold water on your wrists

Running cold water over your wrists activates the dive reflex, slowing your heart rate within seconds.

📵

Phone-free mornings

The first 30 minutes after waking shape your entire day's emotional tone. No social media — breathe first.

🚶

A 10-minute walk works

Research shows a 10-minute walk in nature reduces cortisol and lifts mood effectively.

✍️

Write the worry out

When your thoughts loop, write them down in full. Externalising worry removes it from mental RAM.

🤝

Reach out — even briefly

A one-line text to someone you trust releases oxytocin in both of you. Connection heals isolation.

🌙

Sleep is emotional medicine

Poor sleep amplifies every negative emotion by up to 60%. A consistent bedtime lets your body reset.

🍵

Slow down one daily ritual

Do one thing each day with full attention. This micro-mindfulness rewires how your brain handles stress.
